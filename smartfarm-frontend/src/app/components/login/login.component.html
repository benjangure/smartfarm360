How SmartFarm360 Works

Complete Farm Management Solution

SmartFarm360 is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline farm operations, enhance productivity, and provide real-time insights for modern agricultural management.

Team Management

Manage farm owners, supervisors, and workers with role-based access control.

Time Tracking

GPS-enabled attendance tracking with real-time notifications.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive reporting with productivity insights and trends.

Task Management

Assign, track, and monitor farm tasks with photo proof uploads.

Farm Operations

Manage crops, livestock, and resources with detailed tracking.

Smart Notifications

Email and SMS alerts for important farm events and updates.

User Hierarchy & Roles

System Administrator

Manages the entire system, approves farm owner applications

Farm Owner

Manages their farms, creates supervisors, views all reports

Supervisor

Manages farm operations, assigns tasks, creates worker accounts

Farm Worker

Executes tasks, tracks attendance, reports progress
